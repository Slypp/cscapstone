    Case Study


Recap - Claim:

DREAMer’s Roadmap, is an app that helps undocumented students find college scholarships.

 Explanation:

The CEO, Sarahi Espinoza Salamanca was once an undocumented student looking to attend college but had to drop out of school to support her family. Like her, many other undocumented students have also dropped out of school to support their families and do not attend college because they believe it is too expensive.

Most Effective - Claim:
Creating an app that has information for undocumented students looking to attend college for a better future.		 Explanation:
The CEO, Sarahi Espinoza Salamanca, created DREAMer’s Roadmap as a way for undocumented students to access information to succeed and attend college. Based on her personal experience, she believes that creating an app with information will help thousands of students eliminate the barriers to succeed and achieve their full potential.
Use of Technology - Claim:
The idea of creating an app is great, as most people nowadays know how to use phones and have a phone. By creating an app, it makes it easier for everyone to access the information wherever and whenever they want/need it.		 Explanation:
An app allows for fast and easy access to any information that you need and want and the information is free to use by anyone. The efficiency is also helped by the fact that the interface is easy to use and understand, which helps parents and teachers understand what type of information is being given to them.
Implications for my actual project:
I don’t think it is possible to completely fix the issue as there are many factors, but I do believe it is possible to improve and facilitate the application process for everyone. I believe that creating a broad and reliable source of information will help students during the application season.		 Explanation:By creating a data source for students, it will make it easier for them to complete applications. They will be able to look up whatever information they need; scholarships, colleges, requirements, deadlines, etc. Facilitating the application process, will also help students with stress and will allow them to enjoy their last fall in high school, their last winter in high school, and their spring in high school
